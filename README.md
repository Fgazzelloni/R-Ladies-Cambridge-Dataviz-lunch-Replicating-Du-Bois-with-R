# R-Ladies-Cambridge-Dataviz-lunch-Replicating-Du-Bois-with-R

## Talk for R-Ladies Cambridge

 

Details             | Output
-------------------------|-------------------------
In light of the ongoing **#DuboisChallenge2024**, I'll demonstrate how to replicate one of the original plates from **W.E.B. Du Bois's legacy at the 1900 Paris Exposition** using modern tools such as R. We'll explore the colours used in the plates and delve into the intricacies of the challenge by understanding the perspective of hand-made graphs in the "Duboisian" style. This session can be hands-on or just to watch-learn, depending on your preference.
